Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the AI forecasts?

Forecasts improve over time as we collect more data. We avoid publishing a fixed “accuracy %” because checkpoint traffic is inherently unpredictable. Use our insights as guidance—not gospel.

Do you track my location?

No personal location data is stored. When you vote, we use your device’s GPS transiently to weight votes and then discard it. Nothing is tied back to your identity.

How can I support development?

Watch an occasional video ad, share the app with friends, or buy us a coffee via the link above. Your support funds server costs and new features.
