Frequently Asked Questions

     
       
          How accurate are the AI forecasts?          

Forecasts improve over time as more data is collected. Traffic is unpredictable—so please use these insights as guidance, not gospel.

       
       
          Why allow personalised ads?          

Personalised ads typically generate more revenue, which helps fund the GPU time for the AI and faster feature rollouts. It’s entirely your choice—you can toggle it off anytime in Settings.

       
       
          How can I support development?          

Leaving a review, sharing the app with friends, keeping ads enabled, or sending a small donation—every bit helps me keep SmartCommute running and improving.
